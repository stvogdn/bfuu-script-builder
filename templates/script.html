Joys & Sorrows: Stones & Water Ritual

[SLIDE: Joys & Sorrows / Ritual of the Stones]

Joy & Sorrows Reader: 

The ritual of Joys and Sorrows is a time for each of us to share what is in our hearts this week, and ask for community prayers and support. Type in your words into the chat box, and I will read them aloud for the community to hear.

As I read your Joys & Sorrows, I will choose a stone to represent your joys or sorrows, hopes or fears, questions or prayers, and place the stone into water representing our community’s larger spirit.  Water has the power to connect us wherever we are.

Reads the comments.

Places the stone in water on the screen, one for each comment.
